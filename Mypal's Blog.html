Short Stories and Daily life of Mypal

Who is Mypal?

Mypal is a raccoon keeping old operating systems alive with his cuteness overload.

Hiding in a trashcan

One day Mypal had to go to school. His mother wanted him to bathe, but Mypal didn't want to. So, Mypal hid in a bin just to avoid it and when his mother found him, he looked at her so innocently his mother's heart immediately melted.

Falling off the Stairs

One day, Mypal didn't want to go to school. His mother was annoyed with the fact that Mypal kept skipping school. So Mypal tried to run away from his mother. While doing so, he accidentally fell off the stairs!

Mother, Mypal is Hungry!

One day, Mypal sat to eat. He saw his favourite food cakes, strawberries and blueberries. Mypal immediately started eating during which he looked so cute, it felt like he deserved a hug!

At Dawn We Ride

One day, Mypal woke up in the evening and found a toy. He really liked that toy. So he started playing with it while drinking chocolate milkshake. Then accidentally, Mypal put too much pressure on the toy and he later fell down with the chocolate milkshake splashing and getting smeared on his face!

Pretty Girl!

One day, Mypal came to the garden to play with his friends. One of his friends made a flower garland for his head, Mypal liked it so much he wore it for the entire day!

Ready for Winter!

One day, on Christmas Eve, Mypal wore a cute winter dress. Mypal's mother loved it and kissed him. Mypal said, 'Mommy, where are the cookies?', to which she replied 'In the cupboard, dear!'

Stay Away From My Son!

One day, Mypal came close to a horrifying encounter. A poacher tried to kidnap him and take him away. Mypal became afraid. Mypal's mother came in to protect her baby from the poacher.
